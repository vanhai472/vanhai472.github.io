***What is climate change?

The current situation We know that climate change has been affecting ecosystems on Earth and directly impacting people's daily lives.

So what is climate change?

October 13, 2019

Climate change is a change of a climate system of the present and future atmosphere, atmosphere, biosphere, lithosphere, and bench tape by natural and artificial causes in a certain period, in decades or millions of years. The change may be a change in average weather or a change in the distribution of weather events around an average.
