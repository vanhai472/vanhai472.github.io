Solution

October 13, 2019

Favorable transportation system will contribute in reducing the amount of emissions emitted by vehicles into the environment. Industrial zones need scientific planning, handling of exhaust gases to reduce the amount of environmental pollution.

Limiting the use of fossil fuels: Using fossil fuels such as coal, oil, natural gas ... Causing great greenhouse effect. Therefore, to overcome climate change, it is necessary to find safe solutions or use safe alternative fuel sources such as biofuels, etc.

Reducing spending: Reducing spending costs will help reduce production activities. Since then, the amount of emissions causing the greenhouse effect of plants is also limited. We can use safe or recycled materials to save on production.

Protection of forest resources and planting of trees: Currently, deforestation is increasing not only in our country but also in the world. Therefore, preventing deforestation will help protect natural resources. In addition, we need to raise awareness, plant trees, do not discharge waste to the environment to protect this valuable natural resource.

Saving electricity and water: Saving electricity will help reduce environmental pollution quite effectively. People can use electrical appliances economically and switch off when not in use. In addition, water is not an infinite resource. We need to use water efficiently to not deplete our natural resources.

Exploiting new sources of energy: Natural resources will be increasingly exhausted if people do not seek new sources of fuel. With the development of science and technology, experts have discovered new environmentally safe

energy sources such as energy from the sun, wind, heat, waves and ethanol from plants, hydrogen from the process. hydrolyzed water, etc. There should be strong sanctions for individuals, organizations, ... taking actions affecting the environment. Propaganda and education Establishing an organization to support developing countries in the implementation of environmentally sound policies and practices such as: The United Nations Environment Program (UNEP) is an agency The UN coordinates the UN environmental activities.

The United Nations Environment Program activities cover a wide range of issues related to the atmosphere, aquatic and terrestrial ecosystems, environmental governance and green economy combined with policy, development and implementation. policies with national governments, regional organizations in combination with environmental non-governmental organizations (NGOs)
