***What is climate change?

The current situation We know that climate change has been affecting ecosystems on Earth and directly impacting people's daily lives.

Consequences

October 17, 2019

Climate change is a change of a climate system of the present and future atmosphere, atmosphere, biosphere, lithosphere, and bench tape by natural and artificial causes in a certain period, in decades or millions of years. The change may be a change in average weather or a change in the distribution of weather events around an average. Climate change is limited within a certain region or may occur across the globe.

What are the manifestations of climate change?

The movement of climate zones

The intensity of operation of the atmospheric circulation is changed

Biogeochemical cycle and water circulation in nature are changed

The biological productivity of the ecosystem is changed

The composition of the biosphere, hydrosphere, geosphere is changed.
